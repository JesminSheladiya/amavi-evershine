Everything You Need to Know

Common questions from buyers looking for a 2BHK flat in Virar.

What is the price of a 2BHK flat in Virar at Evershine Amavi 303?
2BHK flats at Evershine Amavi 303 in Virar West start from ₹65.07 Lacs (all-inclusive) for a 535 sq.ft. carpet area apartment, and go up to ₹74.48 Lacs for the 612 sq.ft. variant. The all-inclusive price covers base cost, GST, stamp duty, registration charges and possession charges — no hidden extras.
Is Evershine Amavi 303 RERA registered?
Yes. Evershine Amavi 303 is fully registered under MahaRERA: Phase 1 – P99000024780, Phase 2 – P99000024860, Phase 3 – P99000024753. OC (Occupancy Certificate) has been received and the project is ready for possession. You can verify details at maharera.maharashtra.gov.in.
How far is Evershine Amavi 303 from Virar Railway Station?
Evershine Amavi 303 is approximately 2.5 km from Virar Railway Station on the Western Railway line — roughly 8 minutes by auto-rickshaw. This gives residents easy access to all stations from Virar to Churchgate, including Andheri, Bandra and Mumbai Central.
What amenities are available at Evershine Amavi 303?
The project offers 30+ amenities including a clubhouse, gymnasium, swimming pool, kids play area, jogging track, multipurpose sports court, yoga deck, open gym area, amphitheatre, cycling track, landscaped gardens, party hall, 24×7 security, power backup and a sewage treatment plant.
What is included in the all-inclusive price?
The all-inclusive price at Evershine Amavi 303 covers the base cost of the flat, GST, stamp duty, registration charges and possession charges. There are no surprise add-ons. Always ask for the official cost sheet in writing, which Evershine provides transparently.
Is home loan available for Evershine Amavi 303?
Yes. Evershine Amavi 303 is approved by major banks including SBI, HDFC and ICICI Bank for home loan disbursement. This ensures faster loan processing and gives buyers confidence in the project's documentation and title clarity. Construction Linked Plan (CLP) payment options are also available.
Is buying a 2BHK in Virar a good investment in 2025?
Virar is one of Mumbai's fastest-growing residential corridors in 2025. With the upcoming metro line, Bullet Train terminal, and the 140km Virar–Alibaug corridor under development, infrastructure investment in the area is accelerating. A 2BHK starting at ₹65 Lacs in Virar is significantly more affordable than comparable sizes in Borivali (₹1.2–1.5 Cr+) or Andheri, making it an excellent entry-level investment with strong appreciation potential.
