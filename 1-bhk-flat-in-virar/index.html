Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Common questions from buyers looking for a 1 BHK flat in Virar.

What is the price of a 1 BHK flat in Virar at Evershine Amavi 303?
1 BHK flats at Evershine Amavi 303 start from ₹48.20 Lacs (all-inclusive) for a 395 sq.ft. carpet area apartment, and go up to ₹51.63 Lacs for the 435 sq.ft. 1 BHK Premium variant. The all-inclusive price covers base cost, GST, stamp duty, registration charges and possession charges — no hidden extras.
Is Evershine Amavi 303 RERA registered?
Yes. Evershine Amavi 303 is fully registered under MahaRERA: Phase 1 – P99000024780, Phase 2 – P99000024860, Phase 3 – P99000024753. OC (Occupancy Certificate) has been received and the project is ready for possession. You can verify details at maharera.maharashtra.gov.in.
How far is Evershine Amavi 303 from Virar Railway Station?
Evershine Amavi 303 is approximately 3.3 km from Virar Railway Station on the Western Railway line — roughly 11 minutes by auto-rickshaw. From Virar, you can reach Andheri in ~55 minutes and Churchgate in ~90 minutes on the Western Railway fast local.
What amenities are available at Evershine Amavi 303?
The project offers 50+ amenities including a clubhouse, swimming pool, gymnasium, banquet hall, yoga deck, kids play area, jogging track, cycling track, multipurpose sports court, amphitheatre, landscaped gardens, 24×7 security, power backup and a sewage treatment plant — all within the Evershine Global City township.
What is included in the all-inclusive price of the 1 BHK flat?
The all-inclusive price at Evershine Amavi 303 covers the base cost of the flat, GST, stamp duty, registration charges and possession charges. There are no surprise add-ons. Always ask for the official cost sheet in writing, which Evershine provides transparently.
Is home loan available for Evershine Amavi 303?
Yes. Evershine Amavi 303 is approved by major banks including SBI, HDFC and ICICI Bank for home loan disbursement. This ensures faster loan processing and gives buyers confidence in the project's documentation and title clarity. A Builder Subvention Scheme is also available — no bank EMI required until possession.
Is buying a 1 BHK in Virar a good investment in 2025?
Virar is one of Mumbai's fastest-growing residential corridors in 2025. With the upcoming metro line, Bullet Train terminal, and the 140km Virar–Alibaug corridor under development, infrastructure investment in the area is accelerating. A 1 BHK under ₹52 Lacs in a RERA-registered township with 50+ amenities is an excellent first-time or investment purchase with strong appreciation potential.
